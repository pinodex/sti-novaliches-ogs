Frequently Asked Questions

How can I login to my account?

Login to your account by entering your student number and your middle name as your password.

If your middle name contains accented letters (e.g. ñ [enye]), you may use the non-accented one in place of it (e.g. n).

Can I change my password?

You can’t. Unless you change your middle name.

My account is temporarily locked. What do I do?

Don’t panic. Raise your concern to your professor/instructor to get your account information updated.

Why are some of my subjects missing?

Your subject professor/instructor has not updated your account yet.

Why are some of my subjects missing?

Your subject professor/instructor has not updated your account yet.

Why can’t I see my grade in finals?

Grades in finals can be obtained only after the semester when claiming your grading sheet. It is also intended to excite you.

I found a bug. What do I do?

Let’s catch it! Please visit the bug reporting page to report errors, issues, or security vulnerabilities you found.

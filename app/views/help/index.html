Frequently Asked Questions

How can I login to my account?

Login to your account by entering your student number and your middle name as your password.

If your middle name contains accented letters (e.g. ñ [enye]), you may use the non-accented one in place of it (e.g. n).

If you‘re unable to login, please visit the Academic Head Office and look for Mr. Noriel Domondon for assistance.

My account is temporarily locked. What do I do?

Please visit the Academic Head Office and look for Mr. Noriel Domondon to get your account information updated.

Why are some of my subjects missing?

Your subject professor/instructor has not updated your account yet.
